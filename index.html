About Us

BRIMIEG ENTERPRISE COMPANY LIMITED is a locally owned Private Limited Construction and Consultancy company registered in Kenya, No. PVT-9XU3E6, certificate of Incorporation, 19 May 2017.

The company is led by Engineer HELLEN ADHIAMBO OUKO, a professional in the field of operation, who is actively involved in the company operations as Director and manages all Projects.

We have a solid reputation for reliability and professional expertise based on the use of varied and modern construction techniques. Our team is vibrant and dynamic, employing diligent service people who are experienced, undoubtedly skilled and knowledgeable.

Our Mission

To engineer, design and maintain quality, future-ready, sustainable and resilient facilities while offering strategic innovation, cost effective, custom client satisfaction and exceptional quality. In all dealings achieve superior financial returns for the company.

Our Vision
